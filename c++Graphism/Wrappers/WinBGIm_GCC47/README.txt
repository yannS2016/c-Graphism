This is the WinBGIm library compiled with MinGW, GCC 4.7.0 on Windows 7.

Place "graphics.h" and "winbgim.h" in your compiler's "include" directory.
Place "libbgi.a" in your compiler's "lib" directory.

Use the supplied "compile.bat" to help you compile your WinBGIm programs 
from the command line, or make sure to link to the proper libraries in 
your IDE.

Find WinBGIm (including the source code) at:
http://winbgim.codecutter.org/
